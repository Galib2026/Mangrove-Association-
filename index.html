Journey & History

Mangrove Association started as a small initiative of a few students who wanted to create a platform for academic discussion, environmental awareness and strong bonding between different batches.

Over time it grew into a community where students, teachers and alumni work together for seminars, field visits, publication, social work and campus-wide programs.

Mission & Vision
  • To connect current students, teachers and alumni under one umbrella.
  • To arrange academic programs, environment-focused events and social initiatives.
  • To create a structured platform for mentoring, guidance and leadership development.
  • To preserve the memories and history of Mangrove Association in an organized way.

About Mangrove Association

Short overview, history, mission, vision and core values of Mangrove Association.

History

Mangrove Association was formed by students of the Department of Geography & Environment to create an organized platform for departmental programs and social contribution.

What started as small discussion sessions and voluntary work gradually transformed into a structured association with constitution, elected committee and regular activities.

Today, Mangrove Association keeps record of programs, connects alumni, and supports department-wide initiatives through cooperation and teamwork.

Vision, Mission & Core Values

Vision: A connected, responsible and research-oriented community of students, teachers, staff and alumni.

Mission:

  • Organize academic, cultural and social programs under one umbrella.
  • Create leadership and teamwork opportunities for students.
  • Build long-term connection between present batches and alumni.
  • Promote environmental awareness and sustainable practices.

Core values: Respect, responsibility, transparency, collaboration and service.

Why donation is needed?

Mangrove Association is a non-profit platform. To organize seminars, workshops, environmental activities, publications, and emergency support for students, a transparent fund is needed.

Donations from alumni, teachers and well-wishers help to make these initiatives possible. All expenses can be recorded in the finance report according to constitution.
